Process

From brief to production in three steps

SiteSprint replaces multi-week agency cycles with a governed, AI-accelerated workflow your teams actually control.

01
Brief the AI in plain language
Describe your campaign goal, audience segment, and KPIs. SiteSprint cross-references your brand guidelines and historical performance data to frame the right page architecture.
02
Review, refine, and approve
Generated pages enter your approval workflow. Stakeholders annotate directly on the preview. The AI iterates on feedback in real time — no developer handoff required.
03
Publish with built-in guardrails
One-click deployment to your CDN or CMS. Post-publish, the Conversion Intelligence Layer monitors performance and surfaces optimisation recommendations continuously.
